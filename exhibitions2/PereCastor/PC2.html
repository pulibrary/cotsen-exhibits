The beaver, traditionally associated with industry and construction, was chosen as the series mascot. Over the years, the Père Castor books featured a variety of logos with "Father Beaver" in different guises. In 1994 the beaver put on tweeds to star as a single father in the French animated television series "Les histoires du Père Castor."
